Comments on: Data Aggregation for Reporting http://adventuresinsoftware.com/blog/?p=54 Lessons learned on the road to quality computing Fri, 13 Jan 2017 13:02:21 +0000 http://wordpress.org/?v=2.3.3 By: Charles Martin http://adventuresinsoftware.com/blog/?p=54#comment-224 Charles Martin Fri, 17 Nov 2006 04:16:50 +0000 http://adventuresinsoftware.com/blog/?p=54#comment-224 Some data processing systems have a "cost" associated with pulling information from the data warehouse for reporting purposes which is charged back to the client. This cost can play a significant role in which method is less prohibitive in the eyes of the budget ninjas. A large download of data used for number-crunching may be less expensive than hundreds/thousands of small queries running throughout the day. In addition to the budgeting of such transactions, the large download can be timed so that it has a lessened impact on live processing. Overnight updates can be performed much faster when other processing is in a lull, reducing the "cost" of the download even more than a "quick" query running during peak hours.
